Methodology

H0 (null hypothesis): Within each game, a player's shot outcomes are i.i.d. Bernoulli(p_i) — pure randomness, no hot-hand effect.

H1 (alternative): The playoff streakiness (aggregated Z-score Z_PO) differs significantly from the regular-season baseline (Z_RS), i.e. DeltaZ = Z_PO - Z_RS != 0.

Test statistic: Volume-weighted Wald-Wolfowitz Z-score per game, meta-analytically aggregated. Negative Z means fewer runs than expected (streaky / hot-hand). Positive Z means more alternating than expected (anti-streaky).

p-value: Two-tailed empirical p-value from a game-level parametric bootstrap (B = 5 000). Significance threshold: alpha = 0.05.
